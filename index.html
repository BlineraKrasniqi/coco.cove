Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 14:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request and subject to availability for a nominal fee.

Is parking available at the resort?

Yes, we offer complimentary self-parking and valet parking services for all our guests.

Do you have pet-friendly accommodations?

Unfortunately, we do not allow pets at the resort, with the exception of service animals with proper documentation.

What dining options are available?

Coco Cove features three distinct dining venues: The Ocean's Catch (seafood), Azure Fine Dining (international gourmet), and The Splash Cafe (casual bites and drinks). We also offer 24/7 in-room dining.
